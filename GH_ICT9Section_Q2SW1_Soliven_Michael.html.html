Top 3 Most Popular Dog Breeds (2022)






Click here for a longer list
Labrador Retriever
The Labrador Retriever is currently the most popular dog breed of 2022. Their life expectancy lasts between 10-12 years. They are medium to large in size, being 21.5-24.5 inches in height. They are good with children, seniors, other breeds of dogs and cats making them an ideal breed for any family. The reason why they are good with families is because they are friendly and playful most of the time. Another reason is because they are easy to train, groom, and prone to health issues, making them an ideal breed for first-time owners. They are very active dogs and the need for them to exercise is high.
French Bulldog
Coming it at second place in it's short legs is the French Bulldog. Currently, they are the most popular SMALL breed of dog, with them being 11 to 13 inches in height. Their life expectancy is the same as the Labrador Retriever, it being 10-12 years. They aren't aggressive and are relaxed but they also love to play. They are very good as companion pets however they need to be socialized properly first. These dogs can be mischievous however so you need to have some patience in them while also having fun. They have some common health risks however such as Brachycephalic Obstructive Airway Disease so please handle them with care. They also have lower exercise requirements compared to the Labrador Retriever.
German Shepherd
The German Shepherd dog has went down the rankings since 2019 but still remains strong at third place. They have a life expectancy of 9-13 years, and are larger dogs with them being 22 to 26 inches. These are brave and loyal dogs that are very intelligent. They are also good with families and are playful and gentle dogs. Their exercise needs are high like the Labrador. They are also tolerant to cold weather because of their fur.
